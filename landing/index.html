What's inside

8 training modes, all powered by live data

Every session uses vulnerabilities being actively exploited right now — not examples written years ago.

01

Daily Threat Briefing

AI-generated morning SITREP pulled from live CISA KEV and government alert feeds.

02

CVE Explorer

Browse today's critical vulnerabilities from NIST NVD and deep-dive any CVE with AI analysis.

03

Active Exploits

CISA Known Exploited Vulnerabilities — the ones being weaponised against real organisations right now.

04

Threat Hunt Scenario

Guided hunt walkthroughs with SIEM queries, attacker decision trees, and detection strategies.

05

Concept Explainer

Ask about any security concept or framework and get a practitioner-level SOC breakdown.

06

Knowledge Quiz

Scored multiple-choice questions on any topic, with detailed explanations for every answer.

07

KEV Catalog Stats

Top targeted vendors, ransomware trends, and a full overview of CISA's exploit catalog.

08

IRIS — Incident Response Simulator

Multi-turn attack scenarios. A real incident is unfolding. You make the calls. Your decisions have consequences.
